The Forum

The Aurora Forum exists to unite ambitious youth through a distinguished network that cultivates leadership, resilience, and vision. We believe that true influence is born not in isolation, but through purposeful connections that inspire growth and collaboration — hosting forums, camps, MUNs, and service initiatives where young individuals refine communication, forge alliances, and rise as leaders of integrity.

"guided by the twin pillars of networking and leadership, we pledge to create a community where young minds may converge, connect, cultivate the skills that define tommorows leaders."

— Aurora Forum Charter, Preamble
Membership

A Forum for All Minds

Open to all students between the ages of fourteen and twenty-two. No prior experience in debate, journalism, or diplomacy is required — only a willingness to engage earnestly with the world's great questions.

Weekly workshops cover public speaking, research methodology, and parliamentary procedure, ensuring that every member arrives at the table prepared.

Looking Ahead

What Lies Ahead

Plans are underway for an expansion of the forum's publication wing, with a quarterly print edition of this very journal to be circulated among partner schools.

Collaborations with international youth organisations are in negotiation, and the forum's first fully-fledged Model United Nations conference is set to be announced — should one be cleared to read it.

Special Report

Aurora MUN — First Edition

A conference of ambition, resolution & purpose

The inaugural Aurora Model United Nations conference promises to bring together delegates from across the region for three days of intensive committee sessions. Committees will cover the Security Council, UNHCR, and a special crisis committee.

Registration details, committee topics, background guides, and delegate pricing will be released through official channels. A secretariat of twelve has been appointed, working in strict confidentiality until the formal launch date arrives.

About the Forum

Who we are · What we stand for · Where we go

The Aurora Forum is a youth-led organisation dedicated to the belief that thoughtful debate, rigorous research, and compassionate dialogue are the foundations of a better world. We were established in 2025 by a small group of students who found no existing space that matched their ambition — and so they made one.

Our name was chosen with care. The aurora — that breathtaking phenomenon of light at the edges of the world — speaks to what we aspire to be: a brilliant, fleeting illumination at the horizon of possibility. We are young, and we are reaching.
